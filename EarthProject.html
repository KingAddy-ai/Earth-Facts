A few facts about the Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life,
A status enabled by its stable liquid water, oxygen-rich atmosphere, and protective magnetic field.
It formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago from a swirling cloud of gas and dust,
A process that also gave rise to the entire solar system.

About the Earth

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Mass: 5.972 * 10^24 kg
  • Orbit: 365.25 days
  • Moons: 1 (the Moon)
